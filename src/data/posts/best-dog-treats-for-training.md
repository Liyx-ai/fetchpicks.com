### Training Treat Tips

1. **Size** - Less than 5 calories per treat.
2. **Smell** - Stronger is better. Dogs decide what's worth working for by smell.
3. **Soft texture** - Hard treats take too long to chew. Soft = faster reward delivery.

---

## FAQ

**How many training treats per day?**

Treats should be no more than 10% of daily calories.

**My dog isn't motivated by treats.**

Try higher-value options like freeze-dried liver. Some dogs prefer toys.
