### When to Use Which

**Use a collar when:**
- Your dog walks calmly on a loose leash
- You only need a place for ID tags
- Your dog is being trained for off-leash reliability

**Use a harness when:**
- Your dog pulls
- Your dog has a sensitive trachea (Poms, Yorkies, Chihuahuas)
- Your dog tries to escape the collar
- You need more control in public

**Never use on a collar:** retractable leash + collar (most dangerous combination), choke chains (if misused), or prong collars without professional guidance.

## FAQ

**Is it cruel to walk a dog on a collar?**

Not inherently, but pulling on a collar risks tracheal damage, eye pressure increase, and neck injuries. Switch to harness if your dog pulls.

**Can I use both?**

Yes! Many owners have the dog wear both: harness for walking, collar for ID tags.
