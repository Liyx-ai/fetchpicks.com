### Complete Puppy Checklist

**Buy Before Puppy Arrives:**
- Crate with divider (MidWest iCrate)
- Puppy food (same brand as breeder/shelter — transition slowly)
- Stainless steel bowls
- Collar + ID tag + leash
- Puppy pads + enzymatic cleaner (Nature's Miracle)
- Washable bed + Kong + Nylabone

**Nice to Have:**
- Exercise pen (playpen)
- Gate (block off rooms)
- Grooming tools (brush, nail clippers)
- Harness (skip collar-only walks for puppies)
- Training treats (small, soft)

## FAQ

**What size crate?**

Get adult size with divider. Enough room to stand, turn, lie down.

**First year cost?**

$1500-4000: adoption fee, supplies, vet, food, training.
